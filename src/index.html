About Me

I am primarily a Front-end React Developer who spent years in the class room as an educator. This experience as a Band Director gave me a plethora of unique skills that I apply daily as a developer such as: communication, punctuality, ingenuity, and tenacity. I enjoy to write clean, succinct code that functions well to create vast projects. The design aspect of web development is incredibly enjoyable for me. I love learning which comes in handy when I am learning new technologies or improving my skills.

I have contributed to the ReactJS Dallas open source website which serves around 600 members currently. Most of my education has come from freecodecamp, Udemy.com, and mentorship from several established developers in the area.

I hope to find a collaborative team to work alongside where I can grow and contribute. I am passionate about learning and education and wish to carry that passion with me as I continue to improve my skillset. I also hope to make a positive and impactful difference in the world through my work in the field of web development.
